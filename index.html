Design Concept

The Trick or Thrift logo combines playful typography with bold colors to represent the excitement of thrift shopping. The design balances fun and professionalism, appealing to both bargain hunters and style-conscious shoppers.

Design Philosophy

Our logo represents the "trick" of finding hidden gems and the "thrift" of sustainable shopping. The playful yet clean design communicates our brand's personality: fun, accessible, and eco-conscious.

Design Elements Breakdown

Each element in our logo tells a story

Question Mark Symbol

A question mark to suggest the "mysterious" mix of diverse items that spark curiosity.

Stars Element

Stars scattered around represent the hidden gems and good finds, adding a touch of sparkle to the thrill of the hunt.

Apparel Symbols

Symbolizes the diverse clothing items customers would find in our store, from everyday wear to special finds.

Stitches Design

The stitches represent how we mend and restore pre-loved treasures, giving them new life and value.

Shopping Bag with Piggy Bank

A shopping bag featuring a piggy bank motif to emphasize affordability and smart shopping decisions.

Additional Apparel Symbol

Represents the variety of fashion items available, showcasing our commitment to diverse style options.
